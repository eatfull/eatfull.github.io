COCO Bubble Tea

In 1997, Chairman & Co-Founder Mr. Hung noticed a declining trend in the traditional tea growing and trading business as the tastes and habits of the younger generations evolved. In response, Chairman Hung joined forces with Director Kuo, a fourth generation tea grower in the Dansui area, and Director Chen, then a young man full of ambitions and ideals, to open the first CoCo store in Dansui, which has now grown into a picturesque sea-port town outside of the bustling Taipei City.

For the future, CoCo will continue to dedicate itself to innovation and improvements of operations in order to build an even more sustainable and profitable business. We will continue to develop new product lines, modernize the customer experience, and broaden the accessibility to our brand, thus further penetrating into the worldwide markets and becoming truly the leading brand of fresh food and beverage chain stores in the world.
